Numera 0.16 — insights, pricing & polish

• New: Recurring insights (Numera Pro) — your total recurring spend, a shortcut into the rules manager, and a monthly calendar marking every day a bill or subscription lands.
• New: Budget insights (Numera Pro) — how much of your monthly budget is left, over a tappable history of recent periods.
• Subscription prices now always match your App Store country and currency (no more placeholder prices).
• The account-deletion flow now explains that deleting your account won't cancel an Apple subscription, with a shortcut to manage it first.
• Faster, tighter launch animation — the Numera mark now sits right beside the NUMERA wordmark instead of drifting far to the right.
• The full public changelog is now linked from Home › What's new.

Please test: open the app and watch the launch animation (logo + wordmark should sit together, and it should feel quick); Insights › scroll to the Recurring and Budget cards (blurred preview for free users, live for Pro — tap the recurring calendar and a budget history bar); the paywall shows real prices for your region; Settings › Delete account shows the subscription notice; and Home › What's new? › "View full changelog" opens the web page.
