Numera 0.16.4 — Subscriptions & Insights polish

• New: manage your plan right from the subscription page. Your current plan is marked "Current", and you can Upgrade, Downgrade, or Manage/cancel without leaving the app — subscribers no longer get bounced straight to the system sheet.
• Fixed: the Insights spending donut now shows every category in its own color (matching the list below it), instead of merging everything past the top 5 into one gray slice.
• Improved: after confirming your email on a new account, you now land on a friendly "You're verified" page instead of a blank localhost page.

Please test:
1) While subscribed, open Settings → Subscription: your plan shows "Current", and the button reads Manage / Upgrade / Downgrade as you tap each plan. Tap Manage and confirm the system subscription sheet opens.
2) In Insights, tap categories below the 5th in the breakdown list and confirm the donut highlights that exact category's own color (not a gray "Other" slice).
3) Create a brand-new account and tap the email verification link — confirm it opens the Numera "You're verified" welcome page.
