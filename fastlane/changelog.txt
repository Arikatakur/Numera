Numera 0.13 — native chrome & smarter charts

• New native Apple tab bar (Liquid Glass on iOS 26) — smoother tab switching.
• Glass controls everywhere: chips, pills, the + button and budget cards.
• Insights: tap a donut slice to see that category's spend; tap a bar to focus that month inside its card (the page month no longer jumps).
• "Income left" % never goes negative — it clamps at 0%.
• Tap the average value on the Activity chart to see how it's calculated.
• Edit existing category limits from the pencil badge or long-press menu.
• Page titles now align with the content margin.
